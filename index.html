MEMES FOR DREAMS

Saving lives one meme at a time

What is Memes for Dreams?

Memes for Dreams is a platform for anyone to upload memes for the world to see. The difference between Memes for Dreams and any other meme website is that Memes for Dreams is also a charity.

Instead of liking or upvoting a meme, users donate to a meme. Donations can be as little as one cent to as much as the user is capable of donating. The best part is the user that sumbits their meme gets to choose which charity recieves the money.

Why should I sign up?

Think about all the countless hours you spend looking at memes and wasting your time on the internet. Instead of wasting away like the fat worthless fuck you are, you could at least help someone less fortunate than you!

Why are you still reading this? Sign up and do something useful with your life.
